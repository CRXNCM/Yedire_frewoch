Frequently Asked Questions

Most of our volunteer opportunities require volunteers to be at least 16 years old. However, we do have family-friendly events where younger volunteers can participate when accompanied by an adult. Please contact us for specific age requirements for each opportunity.

Most volunteer positions don't require special qualifications - just enthusiasm and reliability. For certain roles like tutoring or specialized skills, relevant experience is helpful. We provide training for all volunteers regardless of their background.

Time commitments vary by role. Some opportunities require regular weekly commitments, while others are one-time or occasional events. We work with your schedule to find opportunities that fit your availability.
