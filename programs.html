Our Programs

Yedire Frewoch offers a variety of programs designed to address critical needs in our community. Each program is powered by dedicated volunteers committed to making a difference.

Program Categories

Our programs are organized into key areas to better serve our community's diverse needs.

Education

Supporting learning and development for all ages

Health & Wellness

Promoting physical and mental wellbeing

Community Development

Building stronger neighborhoods together

Our Current Programs

After-School Tutoring

Our after-school tutoring program provides academic support to students in grades 1-12. Volunteers work with students on homework, study skills, and specific subject areas where they need additional help.

Program Details:
  • Schedule: Monday-Thursday, 3:00-5:30 PM during the school year
  • Location: Community Center and partner schools
  • Volunteer Needs: Tutors in all subjects, especially math, science, and reading
Volunteer as a Tutor

Community Garden Initiative

Our community gardens provide fresh produce for local families while creating beautiful green spaces in urban areas. Volunteers help with planting, maintenance, harvesting, and educational workshops.

Program Details:
  • Schedule: Year-round with seasonal activities; weekend workdays twice monthly
  • Location: Three garden sites throughout the city
  • Volunteer Needs: Gardeners, educators, construction helpers
Join the Garden Team

Senior Companion Program

Our Senior Companion Program connects volunteers with elderly community members who may be experiencing isolation. Volunteers provide companionship, help with light tasks, and ensure seniors remain connected to the community.

Program Details:
  • Schedule: Flexible, typically 2-4 hours per week
  • Location: In-home visits and community outings
  • Volunteer Needs: Compassionate individuals for regular visits
Become a Companion

Emergency Response Team

Our Emergency Response Team mobilizes quickly when disaster strikes. Volunteers assist with immediate relief efforts, cleanup, and rebuilding after floods, fires, or other emergencies in our community.

Program Details:
  • Schedule: On-call basis with monthly training sessions
  • Location: Throughout the region as needed
  • Volunteer Needs: Team members with various skills, especially construction, first aid, and logistics
Join the Response Team
